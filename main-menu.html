Games

Game Description
Rock-Paper-Scissors

You pick either rock, paper, or scissors, and your oppenent (the computer) does the same. Scissors beats paper, paper beats rock, and rock beats scizzors.
Guess the Number

The computer will select a random number from 1 to 100. You can then input a number, and you will be told whether it is greater than or less than the target number. You win the game if you can succesfully guess the number within 10 tries. You lose if you get it wrong 10 times.
